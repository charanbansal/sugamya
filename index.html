About Us

OUR AIM-The attainment of the highest possible level of services and integration of cooperative societies and society at large through various modes of latest developments for the benefit of society as well as members thereto.

SUGAM, A product of Sugamya Integrated Development Co-Operative Society Limited will help the co-operative societies in deciding whether to give loan to a particular person or not. Whenever any person will come to seek a loan, it can be known whether or not he/she is a member or loan-defaulter in any other society by entering his Aadhar, PAN and Phone Number. The amount of the outstanding loan along with his/her basic details will also be available on your Screen.
